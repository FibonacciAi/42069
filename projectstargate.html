CIA Documents Reveal: Life on Mars

December 23, 2024

Project Stargate Revelations

A declassified CIA document from 1984 suggests evidence of ancient life on Mars through an unusual method: astral projection. The report, part of the classified Project Stargate, claims to have found traces of civilization dating back one million years BC.

The experiment's subject reported viewing pyramids, large roads, and a civilization of "very large people" who were searching for a new home due to environmental corruption.

Key Findings

► Ancient Structures: Pyramids and monuments similar to Egyptian architecture

► Indigenous Population: Tall, thin beings wearing fitted, silk-like clothing

► Environmental Crisis: Evidence of severe storms and environmental deterioration

► Mass Exodus: The civilization allegedly sought refuge elsewhere due to their failing environment

Project Background

Project Stargate was a U.S. Army initiative established in 1977, focusing on psychic phenomena including remote viewing and telepathy. The program, based at Fort Meade, Maryland, recruited individuals claiming to have extrasensory perception (ESP) abilities.

The project continued until 1995, involving various operations from hostage location to criminal tracking. The Mars exploration document was declassified in 2017.
