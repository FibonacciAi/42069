What is Betavolt?

Betavolt has introduced a revolutionary nuclear battery capable of delivering 50 years of continuous power. Utilizing Nickel-63 isotopes, this battery provides energy for decades without recharging.

  • Compact Size: Smaller than a coin, ideal for miniaturized tech.
  • Eco-Friendly: Converts decayed isotopes into non-radioactive copper.
  • Applications: Drones, AI, medical devices, and microprocessors.
