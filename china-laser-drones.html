Breakthrough in Laser Drone Technology

Under the leadership of Li Xiao ("Crazy Li"), China has unveiled drones equipped with cutting-edge laser systems capable of precision strikes and industrial-grade power outputs. This breakthrough stems from a combination of advancements in laser reflection systems and miniaturized targeting technologies.

  • Power Output: Near-infrared laser delivering 30kW+ power via a drone-mounted reflection array.
  • Energy Efficiency: Ground-based lasers feed energy to drones, drastically reducing onboard power requirements.
  • Military Capability: These drones can cut through metal, potentially neutralizing enemy infrastructure and vehicles.

The revelation comes amid rising tensions in the South China Sea, where these drones may provide China with strategic leverage. Additionally, the United States is accelerating its development of counter-drone technologies in response.

  • Modular Designs: Drones can switch between laser targeting, surveillance, and electronic warfare modes.
