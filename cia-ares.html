Mars Exploration, 1984

Declassified CIA documents detail a fascinating 1984 experiment involving astral projection to explore the surface of Mars. Subjects described seeing ancient structures and tall beings who seemed to be struggling with an environmental crisis.

"I saw a large, pyramid-like structure, weathered and worn. It seemed like an ancient civilization existed here."

Key Findings

► Ancient Structures: Subjects described pyramid-like formations and monolithic structures.

► Environmental Collapse: Evidence of a deteriorating environment leading to a mass exodus.

► Tall Beings: The subjects observed "very tall, thin" humanoid beings in distress.

Project Stargate

Part of the CIA's Project Stargate, this experiment utilized individuals trained in astral projection to explore distant locations. It sought to uncover hidden truths through remote viewing techniques.

Declassified in 2017, these findings raise questions about the potential existence of ancient extraterrestrial civilizations and their struggles.
